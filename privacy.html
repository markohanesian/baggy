Privacy Policy & Terms

Effective Date: January 2026

Data Collection & Storage

Baggy is designed with a "privacy-first" architecture. The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All text strings are stored exclusively within your Google Chrome profile via the chrome.storage.sync API.

Important Security Warning

Users are strictly advised not to store highly sensitive information. While synced via Google, this is not a dedicated vault. Avoid storing:

  • Social Security Numbers (SSN)
  • Passwords or PINs
  • Financial or Credit Card details
